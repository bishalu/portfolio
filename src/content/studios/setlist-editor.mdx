## The Problem

Auto-generated sets are useful, but curators still need precise control. One manual swap can break harmonic flow or energy shape.

## The Magic

Editor gives you direct control while the system guards musical continuity in the background. You edit freely, and the model keeps the set coherent.

## The Tech

- Real-time constraints for BPM, key, and energy continuity
- Rule-aware re-ranking when tracks are inserted or removed
- Fast validation to keep editing interactive
